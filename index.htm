Welcome Everyone to Action Soccer

Action Soccer was build by a group of friend who really wanted to bring soccer to their community. Over the course of the last year this small orgainzation has increase to 330 member. With in the same time span Action soccer has hosted countless of scrimmages and practices for the community. Newcomers are alwasy welcome to come and join us for scrimmages. After all Action Soccer was created for everyone to join.

Competitive Soccer

At the moment we have one Elite Team. If any newcommers wish to joing the elite team please keep in mind that everyone on the elite team is much more competitive. If you want to join you must be willing to push yourself to the limit each game, show up for practices or scrimmages. You much also be willing to be yelled at. The Elite Team does have coaches and you must be willing to listen to the feedback they give you. We are COMPETITVIE.
